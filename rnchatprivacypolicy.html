Privacy Policy for RN Chat

Last updated: December 2024

RN Chat, developed by RN Chat, is a mobile application designed for chatting, sharing media such as images, videos, and contacts, creating groups, broadcasting messages, and managing user profiles. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the confidentiality of any information you provide. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data.

Data Collection and Usage

RN Chat collects and uses the following data:

  • Contacts: To help you connect with friends and colleagues, RN Chat requires access to your phone's contact list. We check if the contacts are registered with RN Chat to allow you to chat with them. If they are not registered, we offer an option to send them an invitation.
  • Profile Information: You can create and manage your profile, including your name, photo, and status. This information will be visible to other users within the app.
  • Media Sharing: RN Chat allows users to share images, videos, audio, and contacts with others. The shared media will be stored temporarily for transmission purposes and will not be used for any other purpose.
  • Chat History: RN Chat stores your chat history, including messages and media, on your device. You can manage, delete, or clear your chat history as needed.
  • Group and Broadcast Messaging: RN Chat allows users to create groups and broadcast messages. Information within groups and broadcast messages is accessible to members of those groups or recipients of the broadcast messages.

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function properly:
  • Contacts: To access your phone's contacts and identify registered users with whom you can chat.
  • Camera: To capture and share images or videos directly within the chat.
  • Photo Library: To select and share images from your gallery within the chat.
  • Microphone: To record and share audio messages within the chat.
  • Notification: To send notifications about new messages, invitations, and updates.

Data Security

We prioritize your data security. RN Chat is designed to protect your data by encrypting all communications and storing user data securely. However, please note that while we take all reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. The information you provide is only used for the purposes described in this policy and to improve the functionality of RN Chat.
