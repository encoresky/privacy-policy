Privacy Policy for RNChatt

Last updated: December 2024

RNChatt, developed by RNChatt, is a mobile application designed to facilitate communication through messaging, media sharing, and other social features. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of any personal information you provide. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection.

Data Collection and Usage

RNChatt is designed to facilitate communication between users and is committed to respecting your privacy. To provide its features, the app collects the following types of data:

  • Phone Number: The phone number is used as a unique identifier for signing up and connecting with others. We verify the phone number through OTP for authentication.
  • Contacts: RNChatt accesses your device’s contacts to help you find friends and colleagues who are using the app. We do not store this information on our servers.
  • Messages and Media: The app allows users to send and receive messages, images, videos, audio files, and contacts. These communications are stored temporarily to facilitate chat functionality and are deleted after they are no longer needed.
  • Profile Information: Users can manage their profile, including adding a name, profile picture, and status. This information is stored in your account and can be updated at any time.

Data Security

We take the protection of your data seriously. RNChatt does not share your personal data with third parties except where required by law. Your data, including messages and media, are encrypted and stored securely. We also make efforts to prevent unauthorized access to your account.

Permissions

To provide our full functionality, RNChatt may request the following permissions:

  • Contacts Access: We need access to your contacts to help you find people you know who are also using RNChatt.
  • Camera and Microphone: Access to your camera and microphone is required for sending photos, videos, and audio messages in chats.
  • Storage Access: RNChatt may access your device’s storage to allow you to send and receive media files, including images and videos.

Use of Shared Media

When you share images, videos, audio, or contacts within the app, this content is visible to the recipient of the message. It is important to note that these media files are not shared outside the app unless you choose to export them.

Group and Broadcast Messages

RNChatt allows you to create groups and send broadcast messages to other users. Any information shared within a group or broadcast is accessible to group members and broadcast recipients. We advise that you be cautious when sharing personal or sensitive information within groups or broadcasts.

User Account Management

You can manage your account by updating your profile information, blocking users, clearing chats, or deleting your account. If you choose to delete your account, all your data will be permanently erased from our servers.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the date of the latest revision will be indicated at the top of the policy. Please check this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.

By using RNChatt, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection and use of your data as described herein.
