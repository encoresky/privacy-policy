Privacy Policy for RN-Chatt

Last updated: December 2024

RN-Chatt, developed by RN-Chatt, is a messaging application designed to help users stay connected with friends, family, and colleagues. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of any information you may provide. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection.

Data Collection and Usage

RN-Chatt requires the following permissions and data to provide its core functionality:

  • Contacts Access: We access your phone’s contacts to help you connect with people who are already registered on RN-Chatt. We use this information to suggest contacts for chatting, sharing images, videos, audio, and other media.
  • Phone Number Verification: We use your phone number to authenticate your account and ensure the security of your profile. Your phone number is used only for this purpose.
  • Media Sharing: You can share images, videos, audio, and contacts with other users on RN-Chatt. We do not store these media files on our servers, and they are only shared with the intended recipient during active conversations.
  • Profile Management: You can manage and update your profile, including your name, photo, and status. This information is stored on our servers to personalize your experience on RN-Chatt.
  • Group and Broadcast Messaging: You can create and participate in group chats and broadcast messages. We do not monitor or collect the content of these messages, except for the necessary technical data to ensure proper functionality.
  • Blocked Users: You can block users to prevent them from contacting you. This action is stored locally on your device.
  • Chat History: We provide an option to clear your chat history at any time. Your messages are stored temporarily for the duration of the chat, but we do not retain these messages permanently on our servers.

Data Security

We take data security seriously. RN-Chatt uses encryption and other security measures to protect your data during transmission. However, we do not store sensitive data like messages, images, or videos on our servers, ensuring that your privacy is maintained.
