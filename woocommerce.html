Privacy Policy for Woo-Commerce

Last updated: January 2025

Woo-Commerce, developed by Woo-Commerce, is a mobile application designed to enhance your online shopping experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of any personal information you provide. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection.

Data Collection and Usage

Woo-Commerce collects data to provide an optimized and seamless shopping experience. The app collects the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: We collect your name, email address, phone number, and address to create your account, process orders, and provide customer support.
  • Order Information: Details of your purchases, including product details, quantities, and payment status, are stored to process your orders and provide order history.
  • Device Information: Information such as your device type and operating system is collected to enhance app performance and troubleshoot issues.
  • Usage Data: Data about how you interact with the app (e.g., product views, wishlist additions) is collected to personalize your shopping experience.

Data Security

We take the protection of your data seriously. Woo-Commerce employs advanced security measures to ensure your personal information is stored securely and protected from unauthorized access. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Permissions

To deliver full functionality, Woo-Commerce may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: To allow you to upload profile pictures or scan QR codes for special offers.
  • Storage: To enable you to save and upload media files, such as product images and invoices.

Use of Shared Data

Data you share, such as reviews, ratings, or wishlists, is used to improve app functionality and enhance your shopping experience. Personal data will not be shared with third parties without your consent, except as required for order fulfillment or by law.

User Account Management

You can manage your account by updating your profile information, viewing your order history, or deleting your account. If you choose to delete your account, all personal data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page, and the date of the latest revision will be indicated at the top. Please check this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.

By using Woo-Commerce, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection and use of your data as described herein.
